First feedback

Mathworks

MathWorks develops mathematical computing software, MATLAB and Simulink. They focus on mathematical usage of programming, but other than that they also research machine learning, deep learning, and many more (AI, data science, optimization). In these fields they are looking for software engineers, compiler engineers, etc.

Their employees stated that the work is not that strict, and everyone is friendly, smart, and 100% collaborative.In essence this company is all about math and developing software for people who use math with their work. Because I love both math and ICT, this is one of my dream companies.

CISCO

Cisco Systems is an IT and networking brand that specializes in switches, routers, cybersecurity, and IoT. They are looking for network engineers, software developers, cyber security engineers, who help them make the world better.

CISCO likes to provide their employees the feeling, that they are appreciated, and they are part of something big. They set a big emphasis on culture as well. CISCO is one of the biggest networking companies, and my experience while working with CISCO products, made me curious about networking.

Bethesda Softworks

Bethesda Softworks is an American video game publisher. They work on several game projects, such as The Elder’s Scrolls/Fallout series. In these projects they are looking for a wide range of IT professionals (AI programmer, Network administrator, System engineer). Their employees confessed, that the company culture is great, passionate, but can be too competitive for its own good.

Bethesda have been making fan favorites and became one of the better known game developing companies. Because I grew up their games, I’m biased, and my dream is to work with them.
